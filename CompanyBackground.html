Company Background

Established in 2024, RT 9 East IT Services LLC is a veteran-owned and operated IT consulting firm dedicated to providing elite technical support and cybersecurity solutions. Founded by Michael Ludwig, a decorated U.S. Army veteran with over 30 years of experience in the information technology sector, RT 9 East IT Services LLC was built on the principles of precision, reliability, and service excellence.

Michael’s extensive career began in 1993, spanning decades of specialized service within the U.S. Army Signal Regiment and top-tier global technology firms like Dell EMC. His deep-rooted expertise includes advanced network administration, infrastructure design, and secure information assurance. This unique combination of military discipline and enterprise-level technical proficiency allows RT 9 East IT Services LLC to offer a level of insight and strategic planning rarely found in the consulting space.

At RT 9 East IT Services LLC, we believe that robust technology is the backbone of every successful business. We specialize in helping organizations navigate complex IT landscapes—from data center migrations and Active Directory management to full-scale systems deployments. Our mission is to bridge the gap between sophisticated technology and seamless customer experience, ensuring your operations remain secure, efficient, and future-ready.
